Vision and Mission

Vision

To create a centre of excellence which will produce cutting edge technologists to cater the needs of Business and Society.

MISSION

  • To provide high quality education.
  • To train the students to excel in cutting edge technologies that make them industry ready.
  • To inculcate ethical and professional values in students for the betterment of society.
  • To inculcate entrepreneurial mindset in students in order to make them job creators.

PEO of MCA Department

  • To recognize and adopt social and ethical issues in computing.
  • To become an effective presenter to work as a responsible team leader.
  • To apply knowledge in order to solve real world problems.
